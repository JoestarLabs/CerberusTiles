<p><i>Cerberus Tiles</i> adds essential Quick Settings tiles and app shortcuts to Google Pixel phones, providing quick access to core settings that aren't readily available by default. Ideal for use with back tap gestures.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li><b>Adaptive Brightness:</b> In-app toggle, app shortcut, and Quick Settings tile</li><li><b>Vibration Ringer Mode:</b> In-app toggle and app shortcut</li><li><b>Overlay Dialog:</b> App shortcut functionality</li></ul><p>The app fills the gap where Google Pixel phones lack quick access to certain core settings compared to other Android manufacturers, offering a lightweight solution for power users who want faster control over their device settings.</p>
